LB broth

For an overnight culture, Luria-Bertani (LB) broth was prepared, autoclaved for 40 minutes, and supplemented with kanamycin. 6 ml of the broth was aseptically transferred to a 15 ml conical tube.

A single colony was then picked from the agar culture plate for inoculation into the tube. The tube was closed, gently swirled, and incubated at 37°C overnight on a shaker.

Key skills applied & strengthened:

- Preparation and autoclaving of LB broth media
- Aseptic technique for inoculating bacterial cultures
- Incubation and growth of bacterial cultures in broth media
