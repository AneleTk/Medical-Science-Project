About the Project

Hi, I'm Tankiso.
In 2022, during the SARS-CoV-2 pandemic, I had the opportunity to contribute to research addressing one of the most significant global health challenges of our time. I fully embraced this through a research project titled “Production and Characterization of a Monoclonal Antibody Isolated from a Convalescent SARS-CoV-2 Patient”, with the aim being to express and characterize the antibody. The antibody could then potentially be used for therapeutic purposes or diagnostic applications in the fight against COVID-19.

Please see below all the key steps I undertook to complete the project.

Transformation

Transformation of 10-beta Competent E.coli cells with light and heavy chain plasmids

Transformation Autoclaving Plating

Culture of a single colony from the agar plate with antibiotic
